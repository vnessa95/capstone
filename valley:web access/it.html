Careers

With silicon valley being one of the top metro areas for technology and innovation, it's no secret that the jobs choices here are endless in the tech field! Headquartered here are major companies like, Apple, Alphabet-formally known as Google, Wells fargo, Yahoo, Visa, PlayStation and many, many more. About 285 of every 1,000 workers in silicon valley are in the tech field, that's larger than any metro area in the U.S. Almost 20% of jobs in the valley are in the tech field. With the help of glassdoor reports I have highlighted some of the Valley's highest paying companies to work for.

Genentech

Genentech was founded over 40 years ago, and is based in South San Francisco. This biotech and pharmaceutical company generates over $10 billion a year. As of 2018, Genentech ranked No. 2 on a list of the most trustworthy pharma companies. A typical employee make about $135,000 a year.

NVIDIA

Based in Santa Clara, the computer company's invention of the Graphics Processing Unit advanced the PC gaming market and "modern computer graphics." Today, the company employs over 10,000 people and boasts an annual revenue between $5 and $10 billion. The average yearly compensation is approximatley $170,068.

AppDynamics

AppDynamics is an application performance management and IT operations analytics company based in San Francisco, California. The company focuses on managing the performance and availability of applications across cloud computing environments as well as inside the data center. Working you here you can expect an average of anywhere between 122,000-182,000.
